COURIER

Welcome to Goxpress Transport Courier Services. This document outlines the guidelines for using our transportation services to send packages and documents between different countries. Please read these guidelines carefully to ensure a smooth and reliable experience

SERVICES OVERVIEW

Goexpress Transport Company provides secure and efficient courier services for sending items internationally. We offer a range of shipping options to meet your specific needs, from express services to cost-effective solutions. from express services to cost-effective solutions.

Sending a package

  • Goto the nearest Goxpress terminal around your area.
  • Provide accurate details of the sender and recipient, along with a description of the contents.
  • Select delivery time, and additional services.

Packaging and Labeling

  • Package your items securely to prevent damage during transit.
  • Label your packages clearly with sender and recipient information, including phone numbers.
  • Ensure all required customs documentation is attached.

Restricted and Prohibited Items

  • Packages like hard drugs, ammunations etc are highly prohibited

Pricing and Payment

  • Receive a price quote based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of your package.
  • Pay offline or at our Terminals which is at your area.

Pickup and Delivery

  • Schedule a pickup or drop off your shipment at one of our Terminal.
  • Our drivers will pick up your packages during the specified time window.

Transit Times

  • Expect different delivery times depending on the chosen Terminal location. Express services generally offer faster delivery.

Tracking and Communication

  • Use our online tracking system to monitor the status of your shipment.
  • Receive email or SMS notifications about your package's progress.

Insurance

  • We offer quality insurance to protect your shipment against loss or damage.

Claims and Complaints

  • In the event of a problem, follow our claims process. We are committed to resolving issues promptly.

Customs and Documentation

  • Prepare and submit all necessary customs documentation accurately.
  • We can assist with customs clearance services for a fee.

Security and Handling

  • We prioritize the security of your shipments. We use advanced security measures and tracking systems to safeguard your items.

Environmental Responsibility

  • Goxpress Transport Company is committed to environmental responsibility. We optimize routes and use fuel-efficient vehicles to minimize our carbon footprint.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and complete information.
  • Follow packaging and labeling guidelines.
  • Adhere to customs regulations.

Service Standards and Guarantees

  • We stand behind the quality of our services. Refer to our service level agreements for guarantees on delivery times.

Feedback and Improvement

  • We value your feedback. Please let us know how we can improve our services. Your input helps us serve you better.

Contact Information

  • For inquiries, assistance, or support, contact our customer service at goexpress@gmail.com or visit our website for more information.
Thank You! for choosing Goxpress Transport Company for your international courier needs. We look forward to serving you with reliability and excellence.
